Nascent Athlete Privacy Policy

The Nascent Athlete Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") was updated on September 1, 2015. Your continued use of the Nascent Athlete Service/Application constitutes your acceptance of the Privacy Policy.

Nascent Athlete is a social network for amateur athletes. It is a focused community of interest comprised of athletes, coaches and trainers. Our goal is to help amateur athletes improve their level of play and guide them through their journey of amateur sports. The information we collect from our registered users is used to provide an open and safe channel for athletes to connect and collaborate with one another, seek advice and share information related to their sports. This Privacy Policy states what we collect and do with the information. The Privacy Policy applies to nascentathlete.com and Nascent Athlete mobile applications.

Information We Collect

When you register with Nascent Athlete, you are asked to provide your full name, date of birth (month and year), a valid email address, your city and state and username, password for sign in. You understand that by registering an account, we and other Nascent Athlete users will be able to identify you by your profile.

We collect all the information you provide such as postings, events, statistics, questions, answers. Any contents you generate are captured and stored.

We also collect information about your interaction with other users, and your interaction with the website.

We log information about the device you use to access the site including the IP address, type of device, browser, your mobile device identifier provided by your mobile device operating system and the name of your ISP or mobile carrier.

What We Do With the Information

The information we collect from our registered users is used to promote advice and information sharing among athletes, coaches, trainers and parents. The information also helps us improve our Service, inform you of new features in our Service, and updates to this Privacy Policy or Terms of Use. We may make connection suggestions to you based on your profile. The log files provide statistics on usage, and trends to help us improve our service and are helpful with troubleshooting issues and debugging.

Your personally identifiable information (PII) collected are controlled and safely guarded by Nascent Athlete, LLC. We will not share your PII with a third party unless we obtain your permission. However, we may disclose your PII when compelled by law or subpoena or other legal process, or if necessary to enforce or investigate violation of our Terms of Service.

Your information may be backed up to an external storage for disaster recovery.

We may use your information to target advertisement appropriate for you.

How Do We Share Your Information

The information you provide are shared with users you intend to communicate with. You are able to control the users you intend to communicate with depending on the type of communications. For example, you can communicate with specific users, in which case only those users will see your message. You can pose questions to everyone, in which case all registered users in the network will see the posting. Generally, your connected users will be able to see your profile.

Some data may be stored or backed up off site at third parties.

If the ownership of the company changes, your information will be shared with the new owners.

How Do I Delete My Information

You can delete your account at any time. Once you delete your account, your information will not be available to the other users. However, your information may persist in data backups.

How Do We Notify You of Changes to This Policy

We will notify you through the Service if this Privacy Policy changes. You will be given an opportunity to view new policy before continuing to use the Service. You will not be able to continue to use the Service without agreeing to the new policy.

Cookie Policy

Cookies will be used to uniquely identify a device and session. We will only use cookies so that the users can maintain sessions and avoid having to re-authenticate.
